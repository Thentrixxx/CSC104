Week 5 Web Layout and Design

Website Layout

Layout is the foundation of any design project. It is how the elements on the page are going to be arranged. The goal is to make information on the website easy to find, read and understand. A well-designed layout makes a great user experience and is intuitive and easy to navigate.

Website Design

Design is the visual aspects of the website. It's the colors, fonts, images - the overall style of the brand or company, etc. The design should be attractive and lead to users engaging with the website because it appeals to them.

Relationship between Layout and Design

Layout and design are distinct parts of the website development, but they are interdependent. A good layout ensures that the design elements are placed in a way that is functional and makes for easy navigation. Efficient design makes the layout appealing and engaging.

Both are crucial for creating a good and successful website. All well thought out layout provides the foundation for a visualy appealing design and a good design enhances the effectiveness of the layout.

Why does good design matter:

  • Good design is intangible
  • The website should reflect the quality of the brand
  • Websites sell the product before the company interacts with the user
  • Builds trust in the brand

"Above the fold"

This term is from the days of newspapers. The upper half of the newspaper/website - whatever people see when they first visit - before scrolling.

Why we need good design

  • Access to the information that the user needs
  • Make a good first impression
  • Allows users to engage with the content
  • SEO benefits
  • Accessibility
  • Website Performance
